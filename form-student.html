Receipt of certificates

The date for receiving the certificates

Receipt by the shipping service through the global shipping company FedEx, "the No. 1 shipping company in the world" within 25 days of paying the shipping fees. Care must be taken in writing the address and responding to the shipping representative when communicating to avoid the shipment returning to the headquarters of the Medics Corporation.
